Examination Portal

I've developed an Examination Portal with a multifaceted approach to support various aspects of exam preparation and administration. Users can engage in test-taking activities, both for practice and official exams. The platform hosts and facilitates the participation in exams, ensuring a seamless and efficient experience.

In addition to exam-centric features, the portal includes a robust Q&A forum where users can post questions and receive responses from their peers or educators. This collaborative environment promotes knowledge sharing and addresses doubts effectively.

To aid users in tracking their progress and performance, a user-friendly dashboard has been integrated into the portal. It provides insights into their strengths and weaknesses, enabling them to focus on areas that require improvement.

It's worth noting that the portal is an ongoing project, with continuous development efforts aimed at enhancing its functionality and user experience. The goal is to launch the platform at LASU, catering to the educational needs of students and educators while promoting an enriching learning environment.

Ticketing Platform

The Ticketing Platform is a comprehensive solution for event management and ticket sales. Users can easily purchase tickets for various events, and upon successful purchase, they receive an email with a unique QR code. This QR code serves as their electronic ticket, which can be scanned using a custom QR code scanner on the event day, providing a hassle-free entry process.

The platform also incorporates secure payment integration through Flutterwave, ensuring that transactions are smooth and secure. This client project aims to streamline event ticketing and enhance the overall experience for both event organizers and attendees. Please note that the link to this project cannot be shared due to client confidentiality.
